<p><p>The flat glass market encompasses various types of glass used in construction and industry. Ordinary flat glass, commonly utilized in windows and doors, is available in basic forms and thicknesses. Float glass, produced by floating molten glass on molten tin, offers high clarity and uniform thickness, ideal for architectural applications. Rolled glass, created by rolling molten glass into sheets, provides a textured surface, often used in decorative applications. Each type serves specific purposes, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
