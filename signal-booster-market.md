<p><ul><li>Analog Signal Boosters</li><li>Digital Signal Booster</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The signal booster market comprises two main types: analog signal boosters and digital signal boosters. Analog signal boosters amplify radio frequency signals, enhancing voice transmissions and providing clearer calls in areas with weak reception. In contrast, digital signal boosters process and amplify data signals, improving internet connectivity and performance for digital devices. Each type serves distinct needs, with analog focusing on traditional voice communication, while digital caters to high-speed data requirements in modern mobile networks, supporting the growing demand for reliable connectivity.</p></p>
