<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Pleural Effusions Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thoracentesis</li><li>Pleurodesis</li><li>Pleuroperitoneal Shunt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pleural effusion treatment market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Thoracentesis** - a minimally invasive procedure that involves inserting a needle into the pleural space to remove excess fluid for diagnostic or therapeutic purposes. </p><p>2. **Pleurodesis** - a procedure that induces adhesion between the pleurae to prevent fluid accumulation, typically done after recurrent effusions. </p><p>3. **Pleuroperitoneal Shunt** - a surgical intervention that creates a pathway between the pleural cavity and peritoneal cavity, allowing fluid to drain and reducing thoracic pressure. Each treatment is tailored to the patient's condition and fluid volume.</p></p>
