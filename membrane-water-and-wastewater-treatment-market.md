<p><p>The membrane water and wastewater treatment market consists of several key filtration technologies: microfiltration, ultrafiltration, nanofiltration, and reverse osmosis. Microfiltration removes larger particles and microorganisms, ultrafiltration targets smaller contaminants, including colloids and some viruses. Nanofiltration further enhances filtration by removing divalent ions and smaller solutes, while reverse osmosis achieves the highest purity by filtering out nearly all dissolved salts and impurities. These technologies collectively address various water quality needs in municipal and industrial applications, enhancing sustainability and efficiency.</p></p>
