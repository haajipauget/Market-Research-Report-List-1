<p><ul><li>Analog Type</li><li>Digital Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft fuel gauges market is primarily divided into two types: analog and digital. Analog gauges utilize mechanical components to provide a visual reading of fuel levels through dials and needles, offering straightforward and reliable measurements. In contrast, digital gauges employ electronic sensors and display systems to provide precise fuel level readings, often incorporating advanced features like warnings and diagnostics. The choice between these types depends on the aircraft’s design, technological preferences, and the necessity for accuracy and ease of use.</p></p>
