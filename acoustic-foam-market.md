<p><p>The acoustic foam market consists of various types designed to enhance sound absorption and reduce noise. Acoustic Wedge foam features a triangular shape, optimizing sound diffusion. Acoustic Pyramid foam has pointed peaks that trap sound waves effectively. Acoustic Eggcrate foam features a convoluted surface, improving air movement while dampening sound. Acoustic Spade foam combines flat and angled surfaces for diverse acoustic treatment. Acoustic Wave foam has a wavy texture for dynamic sound absorption, while Acoustic Grid foam offers a structured design for efficient sound control.</p></p>
