<p><p>The RGB-NIR camera market is segmented into two main types: Line Scan Cameras and Area Scan Cameras. Line Scan Cameras capture images by scanning one line at a time, making them ideal for high-speed applications and continuous production environments, such as in industrial inspection. Area Scan Cameras, on the other hand, capture entire frames at once, suitable for applications requiring detailed images of stationary scenes, such as surveillance and scientific research. Both types leverage visible and near-infrared imaging for enhanced data capture.</p></p>
