<p><ul><li>Cold Paste Type</li><li>Hot Melt Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential waterproofing bituminous membrane market features two primary types: cold paste and hot melt. Cold paste type membranes are applied at room temperature, offering easier installation and flexibility, making them suitable for smaller projects. In contrast, hot melt type membranes are applied as a heated liquid, forming a seamless and durable layer upon cooling, ideal for larger areas and providing superior adhesion and long-term performance. Both types serve to protect residential structures from water intrusion, enhancing longevity and durability.</p></p>
