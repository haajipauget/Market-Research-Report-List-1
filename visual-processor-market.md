<p><strong>The Visual Processor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Independent GPU</li><li>Integrated GPU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The visual processor market is categorized into two main types: independent GPUs and integrated GPUs. Independent GPUs are discrete graphics cards with dedicated memory and processing power, designed for high-performance tasks such as gaming, 3D rendering, and professional applications. In contrast, integrated GPUs are built into the motherboard or CPU, sharing system resources, making them suitable for everyday tasks like browsing and light gaming. The choice between the two depends on user needs, performance requirements, and budget considerations.</p></p>
