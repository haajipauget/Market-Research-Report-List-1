<p><p>The fiber optic adapters market is categorized into various types based on connection configurations. The SC type adapter features a square design and is easy to connect and disconnect, making it popular for data centers. The FC type adapter, known for its robust design with a screw-on mechanism, offers excellent stability and low signal loss, often used in high-precision applications. The LC type adapter, with its smaller size and duplex design, is ideal for high-density networking. Other types include ST, MTP/MPO, and E2000, catering to specific needs and applications.</p></p>
