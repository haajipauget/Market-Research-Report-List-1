<p><ul><li>Platform</li><li>Floor-standing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Weighing System market is categorized into two main types: platform and floor-standing scales. Platform scales are designed for larger aircraft, providing a stable base for weighing and often supporting higher weight capacities. They facilitate easy positioning of the aircraft during the weighing process. In contrast, floor-standing scales are more compact, ideal for smaller aircraft or lightweight applications. These systems offer flexibility and portability, enabling operators to weigh planes efficiently even in confined spaces.</p></p>
