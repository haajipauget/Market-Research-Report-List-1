<p><p>Internal cardiotocography (ICTG) is a specialized monitoring technique used during labor to assess fetal heart rate and uterine contractions more accurately than external methods. By placing a thin electrode on the fetal scalp and an intrauterine pressure catheter, clinicians can gain real-time insights into fetal well-being and the progress of labor. This method enhances the ability to detect any potential distress in the fetus, enabling timely interventions.</p><p>The Internal Cardiotocography Market is projected to expand significantly, with an anticipated compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 6% during the forecast period. Moreover, hospitals and healthcare facilities are adopting ICTG for its superior accuracy, which contributes to better outcomes during labor. Furthermore, the ongoing innovation within the field, such as the integration of ICTG with wireless technologies and telemedicine, is expected to enhance monitoring capabilities and improve accessibility, further propelling market growth.
