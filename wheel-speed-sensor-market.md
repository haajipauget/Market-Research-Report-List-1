<p><strong>The Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Wheel Speed Sensor</li><li>Magnetic Electric Wheel Speed Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wheel Speed Sensor market comprises various types, with Hall Effect and Magnetic Electric sensors being prominent. Hall Effect Wheel Speed Sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect wheel speed changes, providing accurate data for vehicle stability and traction control systems. In contrast, Magnetic Electric Wheel Speed Sensors operate through electromagnetic induction, generating voltage signals based on wheel rotation. Both sensor types are essential in modern vehicles for functions like anti-lock braking systems and advanced driver-assistance technologies, enhancing safety and performance.</p></p>
