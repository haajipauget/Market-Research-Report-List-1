<p><ul><li>Static Cold Storage</li><li>Hypothermic Machine Perfusion</li><li>Normothermic Machine Perfusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organ preservation market comprises three main types: static cold storage, hypothermic machine perfusion, and normothermic machine perfusion. Static cold storage involves cooling organs in preservation solutions to prolong viability. Hypothermic machine perfusion actively maintains organ temperatures and perfuses them with preservation solutions, enhancing viability and functionality. Normothermic machine perfusion keeps organs at body temperature, ensuring metabolic processes continue and improving post-transplant outcomes. Each method aims to extend organ preservation time and improve transplant success rates.</p></p>
