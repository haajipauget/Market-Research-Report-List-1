<p><p>The Temper Mill market comprises different types based on the number of working rolls. The 2HI Temper Mill features two rolls, suitable for thicker materials. The 4HI Temper Mill, with four rolls, is used for thinner sheets and offers better thickness control. The 6HI Temper Mill includes six rolls, enhancing precision in processing thin materials while reducing thickness variations. Additionally, "Others" category encompasses specialized temper mills designed for unique applications, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
