<p><p>Diaphragm mechanical seals are pivotal in preventing fluid leakage in various applications. The market features two main types: pusher type seals and non-pusher seals. Pusher type seals utilize a dynamic O-ring that compensates for wear and maintains contact with the mating surface, ensuring reliability under fluctuating conditions. In contrast, non-pusher seals rely on a fixed design, where the diaphragm does not move, offering simplicity and reduced maintenance. Both types cater to distinct operational needs in industries such as oil, gas, and chemicals.</p></p>
