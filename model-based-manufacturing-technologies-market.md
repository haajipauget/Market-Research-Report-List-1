<p><p>Model-Based Manufacturing Technologies encompass various market types, each serving specific manufacturing needs. MES (Manufacturing Execution Systems) focus on real-time production monitoring and control, improving efficiency. ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) systems integrate all business processes, facilitating data flow between departments. MRP (Material Requirements Planning) systems optimize inventory management and production scheduling. CAD (Computer-Aided Design) systems enable precise product design and development, while other MbM software includes simulation tools and analytics, enhancing decision-making and operational effectiveness in manufacturing.</p></p>
