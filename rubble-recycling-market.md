<p><p>Rubble recycling encompasses various market types, including crushing, screening, and stockpiling. The crushing market focuses on breaking down large rubble into smaller, manageable pieces, making it suitable for reuse in construction. Screening involves sorting crushed materials to ensure quality and size consistency, separating useful aggregates from unwanted debris. Stockpiling refers to the safe storage of recycled materials, allowing for effective inventory management and easy access for future projects, enhancing overall sustainability in construction practices.</p></p>
