<p><p>The TV antennas market is divided into two primary types: indoor and outdoor antennas. Indoor TV antennas are compact, designed for easy installation within homes, and typically receive signals from nearby broadcast towers, making them ideal for urban areas. Conversely, outdoor TV antennas are larger and installed outside, allowing for a broader range and stronger signal reception, suitable for rural or suburban environments. Both types cater to consumers seeking to access over-the-air television channels without a cable subscription.</p></p>
