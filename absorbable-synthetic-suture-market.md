<p><p>The absorbable synthetic suture market is categorized into long-term and short-term absorbable sutures. Long-term absorbable sutures are designed to provide support for an extended healing period, typically lasting several weeks to months, making them ideal for complex surgeries where prolonged tissue support is necessary. Short-term absorbable sutures, on the other hand, dissolve quickly, usually within days to a few weeks, making them suitable for less complex procedures where rapid healing is anticipated. Both types ensure effective wound closure while minimizing the need for suture removal.</p></p>
