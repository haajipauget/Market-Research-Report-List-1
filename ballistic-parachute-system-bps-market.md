<p><p>The Ballistic Parachute System (BPS) is a safety mechanism designed to deploy a parachute in emergency situations, allowing for controlled descent and safe landing of aircraft or other airborne vehicles. The market for BPS includes two primary types: internal mounted and external mounted systems. Internal mounted BPS is integrated within the airframe, providing aerodynamic benefits and protection from environmental damage, while external mounted systems are affixed to the aircraft's exterior, allowing for easier access and maintenance during operation.</p></p>
